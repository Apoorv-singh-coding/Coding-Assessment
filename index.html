About the Electric Transport Guide

The electric Transport Guide is your go to application for personel transport conversion. Wheter you are inteseted in walking, using electric kateboards, scooters, or the electric modes of transport,this app helps you calculate travel time based on distance and also vice-versa! choose any of the options from the navigation menu on the left to explore distance-based or time -based calculations for various electric transport modes.
